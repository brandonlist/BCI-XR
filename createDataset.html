Datasets in MetaBCI-XR¶

Dataset class are sub-class of braindecode.BaseConcatDataset, with some alternation. Normally it have a datasets attribute, a list of BaseDataset. The raw attribute of the BaseDataset are mne.io.Raw object, consisting continuous EEG-data of the recording sessions.

There are also the shared data structure description, a dict object containing information of every raw object, i.e. subject_id, session_id and run_id.

Create dataset using your own data¶

To pack your own data, deifine Offline.dataset.CustumDataset class. mne.io.Raw object is required containing following information:

  1. annotations of trials.

  2. EEG channel names following standard 10-05 montage.
